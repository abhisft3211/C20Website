Investing in Mining, Sustaining the Future.

C20 Investments Limited is a diversified investment company specializing in the acquisition, development, and revitalization of gold mining assets. Headquartered in Abu Dhabi, with active operations in Ghana through its wholly owned subsidiary G20 Investments Ghana Ltd, the company strategically invests in brownfield and emerging mining opportunities that deliver both financial growth and sustainable impact.

C20’s investment philosophy is rooted in creating long-term value through responsible capital deployment, operational excellence, and environmental stewardship. By partnering with global technical experts and adhering to international mining standards, the company transforms underperforming assets into productive, sustainable operations that generate economic benefits for investors, host communities, and national economies alike.

  • Delivering strong returns by turning underutilized mining assets into profitable ventures.
  • Investing with a focus on efficiency, risk management, and long-term sustainability.
  • Expanding a balanced portfolio that combines financial strength with responsible resource development.

Investing in Resources, Creating Returns

Our vision, mission, and values shape the way we invest and operate. By combining disciplined capital deployment with sustainable mining practices, we transform resources into enduring value. Guided by integrity, innovation, and responsibility, we deliver strong financial returns while contributing to long-term economic and social progress.

By combining disciplined capital deployment with sustainable mining and resource management practices, we transform opportunities into enduring value. Each project we undertake is guided by a balance of profitability, responsibility, and innovation—ensuring that growth today does not compromise the needs of tomorrow.

Integrity, transparency, and accountability remain at the core of our approach. We invest with a long-term mindset, leveraging advanced technologies, global expertise, and modern exploration techniques to maximize efficiency and returns. Beyond financial success, we are committed to contributing to local development, environmental stewardship, and social progress in the regions where we operate.

Our Mission

To identify, acquire, and optimize mining assets with disciplined capital investment, advanced exploration, and sustainable operations — ensuring profitability with transparency, safety, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To build a world-class mining investment company that transforms natural resources into sustainable economic value, delivering strong returns while creating lasting community impact through responsible and innovative practices.

Core Values

We are guided by integrity and transparency, committed to sustainable growth, and driven by innovation and excellence. Our approach unites performance with responsibility, fostering long-term value for people, communities, and the environment.

At G20 Investments, we transform assets into engines of economic development, empowering people and building resilient communities across Africa and beyond.
