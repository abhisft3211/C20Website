Protecting your private information is our priority. This Privacy Policy governs data collection and usage of the products, services, software, platform, and websites (collectively, “Services”) provided by C20 Investment Limited and any of our affiliates (collectively, “C20 Limited”). By using the Services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy as well as our Terms of Service. If you do not agree, you should stop accessing the Services.

Collection of Personal Information

C20 Investment Limited may collect personally identifiable information depending on your level of activity and interaction with our Services. We may also collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you. Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected, such as your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, and referring website addresses. This information is used to operate the service, maintain quality, and provide general statistics about usage.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or sensitive data through the Services, this information may be collected and used by others. We encourage you to review the privacy statements of websites you link to, as C20 Limited is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

Use of Personal Information

C20 Investment Limited may use your personal information to operate our website and deliver the Services we offer. We may also use your information to inform you of other products or services, or to contact you for surveys and research about current or potential new services.

We do not sell, rent, or lease our customer lists to third parties. We may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of business partners about an offering that may be of interest to you, but your personal information is not shared without your consent. We may share data with trusted partners for analysis, communication, customer support, or delivery purposes. All such partners are prohibited from using your data for anything other than providing agreed services and are bound to maintain its confidentiality.

Security of Your Personal Information

C20 Investment Limited uses tracking tools such as Google Analytics to understand user behavior and improve our Services. Please note that “Do Not Track” signals sent by browsers may not affect our data collection. Some of our third-party analytics partners may have their own policies regarding “Do Not Track” signals.

We only share browser-level data through trusted vendor tracking. Users can opt out of advertising features via Google or resources like the NAI consumer opt-out page.

We may disclose personal information when required by law (e.g., subpoena, warrant, court order), to enforce our policies, to collect amounts owed, to protect rights or safety, or in cases of fraud or emergency. Information may also be disclosed as part of a merger, acquisition, debt financing, sale of assets, bankruptcy, or receivership. In such cases, we will attempt to notify you, though you may not have the right to opt out of the transfer.

You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials. Do not share your password with third parties. You are responsible for all activity under your account, whether or not you authorized it. Please notify us immediately of any unauthorized use.

C20 Investment Limited secures your personal information on controlled servers protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to Privacy Policy

C20 Investment Limited may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will always post the most up-to-date version on our website. We encourage you to review this policy regularly so you remain informed about how we protect your data.
