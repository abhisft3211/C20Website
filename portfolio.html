C20 Investments Limited, a company established to explore, develop, and operate gold mining projects and mining projects. The company is committed to bring in global best practices to mining industry, ensuring compliance with all mining regulations, and adopting international standards in exploration, safety, and environmental management

Vision

To build environmentally sustainable, socially responsible and technically innovative gold mining company that contributes to Country’s mineral industry and regional / national development.

Mission

To identify and develop gold resources using modern exploration techniques, safe mining practices, and sustainable operations.

TECHNICAL EXCELLENCE

Technical Capability

C20 Investments Limited possesses the full spectrum of technical expertise required to advance mining projects from exploration to production, supported by modern methodologies and industry best practices.

Exploration Expertise

C20 Investments Limited possesses the technical capability to undertake systematic gold exploration activities in compliance with international best practices. The company’s exploration approach includes:

  • Geological Mapping – Detailed surface mapping to identify lithologies, structures, and alteration zones associated with gold mineralization.
  • Geochemical Sampling – Collection and analysis of soil, stream sediment, and rock chip samples to delineate anomalies and prioritize drilling targets.
  • Geophysical Surveys – Application of ground and airborne geophysical methods (magnetic, resistivity, induced polarization) to define subsurface mineralization zones.
  • Drilling Programs – Design and execution of rotary air blast (RAB), reverse circulation (RC), and diamond drilling campaigns for resource definition and grade control.

Resource Evaluation

The company is equipped to evaluate mineral resources through established industry standards and methodologies. Competencies include:

  • Resource Estimation – Interpretation of geological and assay data, 3D modeling, and estimation of tonnage and grade using software such as Surpac, Datamine, or Micromine.
  • Feasibility Studies – Conducting scoping, pre-feasibility, and bankable feasibility studies covering mining methods, process design, infrastructure, and economics.
  • Compliance with Codes – Preparation of resource and reserve statements in line with JORC, NI 43-101, or other internationally recognized reporting frameworks.

Project Development

C20 Investments follows a structured project development pathway from discovery to production:

  • Pilot-Scale Projects – Design and implementation of pilot plants for metallurgical testing and process optimization, especially refractory ores (Pressure oxidation, Roasting, etc.).
  • Mine Planning & Design – Development of pit optimization studies, mine scheduling, and infrastructure layouts.
  • Commercial Operations – Transition from exploration to full-scale mining with emphasis on efficiency, safety, and long-term economic viability coupled with social responsibility.
  • Operational Readiness – Integration of workforce training, equipment selection, and supply chain setup to ensure smooth startup.

Regulatory Compliance

The company is committed to strict adherence to Ghana’s mining and environmental regulations, including:

  • Minerals and Mining Act, 2006 (Act 703) and subsequent amendments.
  • EPA Requirements for Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), Environmental Management Plans (EMP), and reclamation obligations.
  • Health and Safety Standards under the Factories, Offices and Shops Act and international Occupational Health & Safety practices.
  • ESG Principles ensuring operations meet modern environmental, social, and governance expectations.

Technical Partnerships

To complement in-house expertise, C20 Investments has established access to a network of reputable technical partners:

  • Geological and Mining Consultants for specialized studies such as structural geology, resource estimation, and pit optimization.
  • Accredited Laboratories in Ghana and internationally for geochemical and metallurgical analyses.
  • Engineering Firms for process design, infrastructure, and feasibility studies.
  • Academic and Research Institutions for knowledge transfer and adoption of modern exploration technologies.

At G20 Investments, we transform assets into engines of economic development, empowering people and building resilient communities across Africa and beyond.
